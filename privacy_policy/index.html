Isle Bloom Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-12-12

This privacy policy applies to the Isle Bloom app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Dillrander (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include your device's IP address, pages visited, time and date of visits, time spent, and operating system.

Firebase Analytics

The Application uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage and interaction data to understand how the Application is used, monitor performance and stability, and improve gameplay experience. Firebase Analytics may automatically collect app interactions, device identifiers, basic device information, and approximate location (country/region).

The Service Provider does not use Firebase Analytics to collect personally identifiable information.

Google Play Games sign-in and Saved Games

The Application offers sign-in through Google Play Games Services (OAuth). When you sign in, the Application may access basic Play Games profile information to enable authentication. The Application uses Saved Games to store and synchronize game progress data. The Service Provider does not store personally identifiable information on proprietary servers for these features.

Advertising (AdMob)

The Application displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob, including rewarded ads. AdMob may collect device information and identifiers to serve and measure ads and prevent fraud.

Use of Information

The Service Provider may use collected information to provide and improve the Application, understand usage, analyze gameplay behavior, improve performance (Firebase Analytics), and display/measure advertisements (AdMob).

Third Party Access

Only aggregated, anonymized data is periodically transmitted to external services. The Application utilizes:

  • Google Firebase Analytics
  • Google Play Services (including Play Games Services)
  • Google AdMob
  • Unity

The Service Provider may disclose information as required by law, to protect rights and safety, investigate fraud, or respond to government requests.

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of data at any time. Game progress stored via Google Play Games Services Saved Games can be deleted through your Google account settings. Contact: erickfeerreira2@gmail.com

Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all collection of information by uninstalling the Application.

Data Retention Policy

The Service Provider retains data for as long as you use the Application and for a reasonable time thereafter. Contact erickfeerreira2@gmail.com for deletion requests.

Children

The Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

Security

The Service Provider provides physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards. Data transmitted to third-party services is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS).

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use is deemed approval of all changes.

Your Consent

By using the Application, you consent to the processing of your information as set forth in this Privacy Policy.
